How to use it?

First go to the form page and fill it with your informations:
- Adress
- Driver Parameters:
- Maximum beeline is the maximum beeline you are willing to go to find your gas
- Time value is the value you attribute to your time. This is important to know in order to find YOUR optimal gas station. A common time value is 0.5â‚¬ per minute. However, if you are in a hurry you can set it to the maximum or if you have plenty of time, you can select the minimum and the app will guide you to the cheapest station.
- Car Parameters

Then, the app will propose you 3 stations:
- The Closest: closest station to you (in driving time)
- The Cheapest: cheapest station in your area
- The Optimal: Optimal station given your Driver Parameters selected by the GasApp algorithm

You will also see how much money $$$ you will save given your Car Parameters :).

Finally, select the station and let the app guides you there!

Note:
- For each station, the cost of the tank filling is the real cost : Tank Filling + Gas cost for the round-trip.
- In a first time, the App is only available for French Gas Station... Sorry for all non-French folks. I will work to add your country on request!
